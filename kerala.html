Welcome to Kerala – God's Own Country

Kerala is famous for its enchanting backwaters, serene beaches, lush greenery, tea plantations, Ayurveda therapies, and cultural richness. Known as "God’s Own Country," Kerala offers a peaceful blend of nature, tradition, and wellness.

Top Places to Visit in Kerala

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the Venice of the East, Alleppey is famous for houseboat cruises along the beautiful backwaters.

Munnar

A picturesque hill station with rolling tea plantations, misty valleys, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Kovalam

A world-renowned beach destination, perfect for relaxation, sunbathing, and Ayurvedic treatments.

Thekkady (Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary)

One of India’s most famous wildlife reserves, known for elephants, tigers, and scenic boat rides.

Kochi

A vibrant port city with colonial heritage, Chinese fishing nets, spice markets, and cultural shows.

Best Time to Visit Kerala

->September to March is the ideal time to visit Kerala when the weather is cool and pleasant, perfect for sightseeing, backwater cruises, and beach holidays. April–May is warmer but good for hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad. June–August is monsoon season, great for Ayurveda treatments and lush green landscapes.
